Ranking after FIBA Continental Qualifiers

1. USA

The most successful national team in the world of men's basketball, which won medals at all the Olympic Games in which it participated, including becoming the Olympic champion 16 times.

2. Spain

The Spanish national basketball team, managed by the Spanish Basketball Federation. FIBA members since 1934. Winners of the World Basketball Championship 2006, 2019, European Basketball Championship 2009, 2011, 2015, 2022, silver medalists of the Olympic Games 1984, 2008, 2012.

3. Germany

The national basketball team that represents Germany in the international basketball arena. The team was created in 1934. The most successful achievements are the 1993 European Championship gold medals, the 2005 European Championship silver medals, the 2023 World Championship gold awards and the 2002 World Championship bronze medals.

4. Serbia

Represents Serbia in international basketball competitions and is governed by the Basketball Federation of Serbia (member of FIBA since 1936). FIBA recognizes the national team of Serbia as the rightful successor of the national basketball teams of Serbia and Montenegro, as well as the national basketball team of Yugoslavia. The current coach of the national team is Svetislav Pesich.
